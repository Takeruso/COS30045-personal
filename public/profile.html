About Us

This project aims to explore and illustrate the relationship between freshwater abstractions and water availability across OECD countries. Through interactive visualizations, we seek to uncover key trends in water resource usage and highlight their implications for sustainable water management practices.

By presenting clear and accessible insights, our goal is to promote university students' awareness of water resource dynamics within the OECD region. We are committed to providing a reliable and impactful resource for understanding how freshwater trends influence sustainability.
